About us

The London Guide is a not only a website, but a team assembled to make the tourists and visitors of London have a pleasant sightseeing and trip whilst here. On this website you can find describtions about the history of this gigantic city, as well as brief informations about the sights such as parks, museums, modern skyscrapers and historical landmarks. The creators of this website are in love with the English capital and are hoping to be your pocket guide when you are here to experience the atmosphere. We believe that we are the perfect way to discover London.

About the city

London is a city that attracts every type of visitors. From its historical buildings with century-old architectural styles to its modern districts with skyscrapers the city has a lot to offer and we can definately explore a strong contrast in its architecture. With its ethnically diverse population of around 8.9 billion, London is the 3rd largest city in Europe. It is the U.K.'s largest metropolis and its economic, transportation, and cultural centre. London is also among the oldest of the world's great cities, with its history spanning nearly two millennia.

Our seasonal suggestions

The City of London awaits!

Did you know that City of London is a local governmental district of about 1 square mile in the downtown of London where most of the world's leading financial institutions have buildings? This is why it is the primary financial district of the capital and a leading financial centre throughout the world. Definately put it in your schedule as it is an unmissable location!

Christmas in Hyde Park!

The Holidays are close, and when in London, you just can't miss the largest Christmas market in the UK. Held every year, the Winter Wonderland is a Christmas market and festival set in Hyde Park and expects milions of visitors! A great way to put an end of a long, tiring sightseeing day is by taking a last walk in the market with a hot drink in your hand.

Next station: Greenwich!

It doesn't matter if you stay for weeks, months or just a couple of days, Greenwich is just something you just can't miss because of the beautiful panorama! Exceptional location to visit during a sunny morning and to take pictures at the Greenwich Meridian then lay out on the grass and just admire Canary Wharf views with pastry and a fresh coffee!
