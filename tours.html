Join the ride and discover the city's treasures!

We are known mostly because of our tour service, which is a popular way to explore London. We offer customers a complete detailed guide about the locations as well as give them an unforgettable experience on the journey. You can choose out of 4 tours, each showcasing the different faces of London.

Welcome Westminster

This tour is for those who are captivated by the historical side of London and would like to explore the district of Westminster. Passengers receive complete information on the sights such as the Westminster Abbey, Buckingham Palace, and the Place of Westminster of course! The bus tour begins its journey at Tate Britain, takes a stop at St. James's Park, and finishes on Westminster Bridge.

Length of tour: appr. 5 hours

London Calling

In this tour, we go around the famous districts of Soho, Mayfair and Covent Garden, visting the most nominal landmarks like Trafalgar Square, the Chinatown, Picadilly Circus, The British Museum, The National Gallery and more! Of course with such a busy schedule we freshen up by taking a break the largest park in London (Hyde Park). This tour is colorful and an optimal choice for those who are intersted in visiting these landmarks.

Length of tour: appr. 4 hours

The Square Mile

The title is actually a nickname of the district we are exploring in this tour. If you guessed the City of London, you are correct! This district offers a lot to visitors in terms of modern architecture. During this tour we take a look at the most famous skyscrapers like 20 Fenchurch St, the Gherkin, and a lot more. At least on hour of this tour is dedicated to the skygarden which is a free-to-visit location with an unmatched panoramic view of London. Apart from this, of course, the Tower of London and the St. Paul's Cathedral are two historical buildings we just can't miss.

Length of tour: appr. 4 hours

Grass to Glass

This tour is for those who are not intimidated by some extra bus ride, because it takes a while to travel through the city. We begin in the calming green area of Greewich, especially to see the Meridian, and the travelling through Canary Wharf and Tower Bridge to reach the City Hall and the Shard, two modern architectural pieces. As the tour's name, the tour's structure has a strong contrast as well, as we start in the green grass and finish among the grey and the glass.

Length of tour: appr. 5 hours
